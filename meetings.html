Excel Saga

Comedy Parody Action

A wild, over-the-top parody series that follows Excel, an enthusiastic but clueless agent of the secret organization Across, as she tries to conquer the city under the eccentric leadership of Il Palazzo. The show constantly breaks the fourth wall and spoofs anime tropes, genres, and even itself. Its chaotic humor and absurd scenarios make it a cult classic.

School-Live! (Gakkou Gurashi!)

Horror Mystery School

At first, it seems like a slice-of-life anime about a group of cheerful girls living at their school. However, it's revealed that they are actually survivors of a zombie apocalypse, with one character maintaining a delusion of normal school life to cope with the trauma. The series balances dark survival horror with bittersweet character bonds.
