MISSION

Our Purpose & Vision

The Terrapin Anime Society is established for the expressed purpose of encouraging the appreciation and discussion of anime, Japanese Animation, between its members and to bring a better awareness of anime to the general public.

Anyone who is interested in anime is free to join. No applications, no fees, just show up to our meetings!

Deadman Wonderland

Thriller Supernatural Horror

A dark thriller about humans called Ajin, who are immortal and hunted by the government for experiments. Kei Nagai, a high school student who discovers he's one of them, is forced into a world of fear, betrayal, and survival.

12 Episodes ★★★★★

Ergo Proxy

Comedy Isekai Fantasy

After waking up from a 17-year coma, Takafumi's uncle reveals he was living in a fantasy world the whole time. Now back in modern Japan, he tries to balance his outdated gamer knowledge, magic powers, and hilarious misunderstandings of modern culture.

13 Episodes ★★★★☆

GANGSTA

Romance Comedy Slice of Life

A wholesome rom-com following Nasa Yuzaki, who marries a mysterious girl named Tsukasa after a chance encounter. The story explores their sweet and quirky newlywed life filled with lighthearted comedy and heartfelt romance.

12 Episodes ★★★★☆

Michiko & Hatchiko

Shonen Supernatural Action

Classic shonen anime where Yusuke Urameshi, a delinquent teen, dies saving a child and is brought back as a Spirit Detective. With friends like Kuwabara, Kurama, and Hiei, he faces demons, tournaments, and battles that test his courage and heart.

112 Episodes ★★★★★

The World God Only Knows

Comedy Music Supernatural

A group of legendary girls from different eras are resurrected as zombies to form an idol group and save Saga Prefecture. Mixing slapstick comedy with heartfelt drama, the series blends absurd humor with surprisingly emotional performances.
