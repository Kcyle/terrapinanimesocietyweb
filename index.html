MISSION

Our Purpose & Vision

The Terrapin Anime Society is established for the expressed purpose of encouraging the appreciation and discussion of anime, Japanese Animation, between its members and to bring a better awareness of anime to the general public.

Anyone who is interested in anime is free to join. No applications, no fees, just show up to our meetings!

THIS WEEK'S SCREENING

SUNDAY SEPTEMBER 21

Excel Saga

Comedy Parody Action

A wild, over-the-top parody series that follows Excel, an enthusiastic but clueless agent of the secret organization Across, as she tries to conquer the city under the eccentric leadership of Il Palazzo. The show constantly breaks the fourth wall and spoofs anime tropes, genres, and even itself. Its chaotic humor and absurd scenarios make it a cult classic.

26 Episodes ★★★★☆

School-Live! (Gakkou Gurashi!)

Horror Mystery School

At first, it seems like a slice-of-life anime about a group of cheerful girls living at their school. However, it's revealed that they are actually survivors of a zombie apocalypse, with one character maintaining a delusion of normal school life to cope with the trauma. The series balances dark survival horror with bittersweet character bonds.

12 Episodes ★★★★★

Talentless Nana

Psychological Thriller Supernatural

On an island where children with superpowers are trained to fight humanity's enemies, a new transfer student named Nana appears. Though she seems powerless, she's secretly an assassin sent to eliminate her classmates, who are considered dangerous. The series blends psychological thriller and mystery as Nana manipulates and deceives those around her.

13 Episodes ★★★★☆

The Executioner and Her Way of Life

Fantasy Adventure Isekai

In a world where people summoned from Japan often gain dangerous powers, priestess Executioners are tasked with eliminating them. Menou, one such Executioner, hides her true duty under a kind façade as she targets a newly summoned girl with frightening abilities. The story mixes fantasy adventure with moral questions about duty, trust, and the value of life.

12 Episodes ★★★★☆

Turkey! Time to Strike

Comedy Action Parody

This is a comedic action short where anthropomorphic turkeys rise up against humans just before Thanksgiving. The birds, armed with absurd weapons and overblown dramatic flair, wage war in a parody of shōnen battle anime tropes. It's tongue-in-cheek, chaotic, and meant as satire rather than a serious series.

12 Episodes ★★★☆☆
