SUBGROUPS

Specialized interest groups within TAS

ANIMUSIC

Leader: Annie | VP: Louis | Secretary: Adam

Dedicated to anime music, OSTs, J-pop, and music-focused anime. Weekly listening parties and karaoke events.

COSPLAY

Leader: Nikolai

Learn cosplay creation, attend workshops, and participate in group cosplay projects for conventions.

MANGA

Leader: Advait

Weekly manga discussions, reading groups, and manga art workshops. Explore both popular and hidden gem series.

OLD SCHOOL ANIME

Leader: Joseph

Appreciation of classic anime from the 70s, 80s, and 90s. Screenings of influential retro series.

RAINBOW

Leader: Corvidae

LGBTQ+ inclusive space for discussing queer themes in anime and creating a welcoming community.

JOIN A SUBGROUP

Each subgroup has its own Discord channel and meeting times. Join our main Discord server to connect with subgroup leaders and participate in specialized activities!
